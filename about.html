Rock am Ring 2020

Rock am Ring, held at the Nürburgring in Germany, is one of the most important and recognized rock music festivals in Europe and even the world

During its 35-year history as the main event on the rock music calendar, legends like David Bowie, U2, Metallica, Radiohead, Pearl Jam and Iron Maiden have risen to the throne of Rock's Center Stage (now known as Volcano Stage). am Ring, and Slipknot, Die Ärzte and Tool joined the list as headliners in 2019.
